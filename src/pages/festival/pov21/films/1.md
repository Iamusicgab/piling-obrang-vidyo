synopsis: "A woman wanders through shifting landscapes of Cebu—urban streets, forgotten corners, and strange, dreamlike spaces—drawn by signs she cannot ignore. Along the way, she encounters fragments of a broken system: lingering traces of power, silence, and loss etched into the city’s skin. With each step, the boundaries between memory, illusion, and reality blur. What begins as a quiet search unfolds into a confrontation with a truth long buried—she is not just a passerby, but one of many uprooted, erased by forces far larger than herself."
